Project Analysis

Site Intent

Client Goals

The goal of the client is to create an alternative investment in an non-correlated asset class to stocks, bonds, and real estate and reduce portfolio risk through diversification.

The purpose of the website is to:
  • Drawn in clientele and raise awareness about other diversification opportunities.
  • Properly hedging portfolios against new market nuances.
  • Create a CTA (Commodity Trading Advisor) that stands out from other CTAs in the market today.
  • Educate investors on real market risks v.s. perceived risks.

User Goals

  • To learn how to make more money with commodity investments
  • Increase the amount of their retirement income.
  • Reduce the risk in the portfolio.
  • Smooth out the volatility in their portfolio.
  • Protect the portfolio against unanticipated world issues.

Target Audience (Buyer Personas)

Jim is a 50 year old business owner with a college degree and has worked in the oil industry for 20+ years. Jim makes an average annual salary of $150k. Jim has been in the commodity market for about 10 years now and has invested in the past with previous commodity brokerages and has lost a significant amount of money in previous trades. Jim knows you can make money with commodities, but has been unsuccessful at it in the past. Jim has strong republican views, is heavy into capitalism and keeps up with World News daily. Jim is constantly craving new market opportunities and is looking for more educational resources to make smarter trades.

Josh is a 35 year old college graduate who is new to the commodity trading and is looking for educational resources to help him with his trades. Josh likes to start his investments slow and watch them grow at a steady pace. Josh is not a very big risk taker and performs multiple hours of research before investing in new market opportunities. In the past, Josh has been successful in investing in the stock market, but has not yet invested in commodities.
